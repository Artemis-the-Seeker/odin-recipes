Buffalo Chicken Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A zesty twist on a classic dish, this Buffalo Chicken Macaroni and Cheese is guaranteed to be a hit at holiday parties. Tender mouthfuls of chicken, spiral rotini, and tangy mixed cheeses combine for a delicious and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 13 oz whole grain pasta, rotini, shells, or penne
  • 1 lb cooked chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 sm. bell peppers, seeded and diced
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped and divided
  • 2 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup refrigerated blue cheese dressing
  • 3/4 cup 'buffalo wing' cayenne pepper sauce
  • 3 cups shredded cheese, divided cheddar, mozzarella, or a blend
  • 3/4 cup panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
  2. Boil the pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and transfer to a 9x13 baking dish.
  3. Add the cooked chicken, chopped peppers, 3/4 of the green onions, and 2 1/4 cups of the shredded cheese to the baking dish. Mix well.
  4. Combine the garlic, cream cheese, blue cheese dressing, and 1/2 cup hot wing sauce in a food processor. Puree until smooth. Pour over pasta and toss.
  5. Mix the remaining cheese and panko crumbs together. Sprinkle over the top of the pasta.
  6. Baked until golden and bubbly, approx. 30-40 minutes.
