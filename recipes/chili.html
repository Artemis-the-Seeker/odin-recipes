Set It and Forget It Chili

Description

For stunningly little effort, you can have a chili that will keep you happy and warm for multiple meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef, browned and drained
  • 2 x 950 ml jars of salsa
  • 2 pkgs of chili seasoning
  • 2 cans of kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • Shredded cheese and sour cream for garnish, optional
  • Soda crackers, optional

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet. Drain and empty into the slow cooker.
  2. Pour the contents of the seasoning packets on top of the beef.
  3. Add the rinsed beans and jars of salsa. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Cook on high for 4-6 hours.
  5. Serve with optional cheese, sour cream, and crackers.
