Mushroom Chicken Stroganoff

Description

A comforting, familiar combination of creamy chicken and mushrooms make for a hearty meal in autumn and winter months.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 8 oz mushrooms, canned or sliced
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 10.5 oz cream of chicken or mushroom soup
  • 1 pkg of dry onion soup mix
  • Noodles or rice for serving

Steps

  1. Place the chicken in the bottom of a lightly greased slow cooker. Then add the mushrooms on top.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl add cream cheese, cream of chicken soup, and dry onion soup mix. Mix until incorporated and spread on top of chicken and mushrooms.
  3. Cook on low for 4-6 hours or high for 3. Serve over noodles or rice.
